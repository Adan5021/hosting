New Tydy DLC.

Tydy DLC adds a completely new org to Void Bastards along with a dangerous new opponent, a brutal new weapon, three new ships to explore and the chance to gather much needed heart starters to keep your client alive in the dangerous Sargasso Nebula!.

Hardcore "Foon" update.

Studio Blue Manchu has released an endgame update for Void Bastards that allows players to play through the entire game with, well, a spoon fork, aka the FOON, after they beat the game normally. This sparkling utensil can backstab enemies with one hit, requiring players to have the utmost patience and a strong wit to succeed. Foon mode allows you to ONLY use the Foon. No other weapons or devices can be used.
